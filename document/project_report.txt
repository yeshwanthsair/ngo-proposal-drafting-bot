PROJECT COMPLETION REPORT

Project Name:
Women Skill Development Initiative

Duration:
January 2024 to December 2024

Objectives:
To provide vocational training for women in rural communities.

Achievements:
- 250 women trained.
- 180 women employed.
- 40 women started small businesses.

Training Areas:
- Tailoring
- Computer Skills
- Digital Marketing
- Entrepreneurship

Challenges:
- Limited internet connectivity.
- Transportation difficulties.

Lessons Learned:
Community participation significantly improved
program success.

Recommendations:
Increase digital access and transportation support
for future projects.
