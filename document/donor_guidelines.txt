DONOR FUNDING GUIDELINES

Eligibility:
Only registered NGOs with at least 3 years of operational
experience may apply.

Required Proposal Sections:
1. Executive Summary
2. Problem Statement
3. Project Objectives
4. Implementation Plan
5. Budget Breakdown
6. Monitoring and Evaluation
7. Sustainability Plan

Budget Requirements:
Administrative expenses should not exceed 10% of
the total project budget.

Reporting Requirements:
Quarterly financial reports must be submitted.
Impact assessment reports are required every 6 months.

Compliance:
All expenditures must be documented with invoices
and receipts.
